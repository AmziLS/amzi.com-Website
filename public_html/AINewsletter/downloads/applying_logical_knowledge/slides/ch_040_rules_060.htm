  Reporting Rules

fail is used to force backtracking in a rule.

a clause with all anonymous variables always succeeds, allowing a predicate to always succeed.

price1_report(FROM) :-
   flight(FROM, TO, CARRIER, FL, _),
   price1_flight(FL, PRICE),
   tab(2), write(TO), tab(1), write(PRICE), nl,
   fail.
price1_report(_).

price1_report :-
   city(FROM, _, _),
   write(FROM), nl,
   price1_report(FROM),
   fail.
price1_report.
