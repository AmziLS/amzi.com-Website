Applying Logical Knowledge

  Compound Queries

Syntactically goals are linked with commas.
Semantically the comma means logical and.

The variable bindings from one goal carry over to the next.

Underbar, _, is a special variable called the anonymous variable. It binds to anything, semantically meaning we don't care what the value of that argument in a structure is.

How many seats on flights to lax?

?- flight(_,lax,_,_,X), seats(X, SEATS).
