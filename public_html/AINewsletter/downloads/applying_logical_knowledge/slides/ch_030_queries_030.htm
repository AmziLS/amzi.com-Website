  Backtracking

Searching a predicate composed of facts, Prolog looks for the first fact that unifies with the goal.

seats(727,100).
seats(767,300).
?- seats(767,300).
  1. functor, seats, matches with clause #1
  2. arity, 2, matches clause #1
  3. first arguments, 727 & 767, FAIL to unify in clause #1
  4. backtrack to clause #2
  5. functor/arity, seats/2, matches
  6. both arguments unify
yes
