Applying Logical Knowledge

  Relational DB

Predicates whose clauses are facts are just like relational tables:

  • clauses = rows
  • arguments = columns.
flight(bos, lax, delta432, 727).
flight(lax, box, delta276, 727).

seats(727, 100).

passenger(duck, daffy, 437896, address(toonsville, ca)).
passenger(bunny, bugs, 893346, address(toonsville, ca)).

booked(437896, delta432).
booked(893346, delta276).
