  Defining Operators

You can define your own operators with builtin op/3:

op(Precedence, Associativity, Operator)

  • Precedence - integer from 1 to 1200 defining how tight operator is bound
  • Associativity - pattern indicating infix, postfix, or prefix and left or right associativity
    ex - xfx means non-associative infix, fx prefix, yfx left associative infix, etc.
  • Operator - the operator (or a list of operators with same precedence, associativity)
?- current_op(P,A,+).
P = 200
A = fy ;
P = 500
A = yfx 
yes
?- ?- display( a+b+c ).
+(+(a,b),c)
yes

Parenthesis change associativity:

?- display( a + (b+c) ).
+(a,+(b,c))
yes
